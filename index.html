Featured Apps

Hybrid or Cross Platform Apps

Apps that support major phone platforms (Android, Windows, Windows) and work the same in all platforms. These apps are quick to develop and offer similar user experience across all platforms.

Native Apps

These are apps that target one specific phone platform e.g. Android and are highly optimized for maximum speed of execution. These apps are best at leveraging platform-specific features and capabilities to offer the fastest performance to your customers.

USSD Apps

These apps can help target your customers who do not have smartphones. You can provide your customers with digital content/services which can be accessed by dialing a short code on their phones e.g. *234#

Progressive Web Apps (PWA's)

A Web app that also works as a hybrid app. A PWA can be used to target people who have low-end phones with small storage and don't have space to install additional apps.
